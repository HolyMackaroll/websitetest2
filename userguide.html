User Guide

Upon arriving at the homepage, you'll be greeted with an image showcasing Mac DeMarco's iconic stage presence. You can easily navigate to different sections using the menu bar located at the top of the page. Each tab leads to a different page, "Home", "About", "Discography", and "Merch".

Underneath the title on the image is a button directing straight to the "About" page. Below this is a "More From Mac" section, with two service cards directing to the "Discography" and "Merch" pages. At the very bottom is the footer, with the title once more, the rights section, and social media icons.

In the "About" section, Mac DeMarco's journey as an artist is highlighted. This section includes a timeline showcasing key moments in his career. Scroll through his history with pictures and text to learn more about the man behind the music.

At the bottom of this and every page is the aforementioned footer. Along with the User Guide icon, the other integrated social media buttons include Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, Spotify, and Apple Music.

In the "Discography" section, you'll find Mac DeMarco's entire discography listed with cover art and Spotify embeds. Click on any album to play it directly from this website, bringing you the songs of Mac in an instant.

This website has been formatted to be responsive with a few resolutions. In the accompanying image, the Discography section is displayed on an iPhone 11.

Under the "Merch" tab is a selection of Mac DeMarco's merchandise. Within it is a range of products like t-shirts, hats, vinyl records, and more. Items can be added and removed from your cart.

Once you've selected your items, the cart at the bottom of the page will show the number and price of items you've selected, including the total price. Using the "Remove" button, items can easily be taken out of your cart.
